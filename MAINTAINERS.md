# CavrixOS Maintainers

This document defines the core maintainers and steering committee for the CavrixOS project.

## Core Steering Committee
- **Faaiz Johar** (@FaaizJohar) - Lead Architect & BDFL

## Subsystem Maintainers

### CavrixInstall & Boot Architecture
- **@cavrix-installer-team**
- Responsibilities: `installer/`, Btrfs layout policies, UKI generation, LUKS2/TPM integration.

### AI Subsystem
- **@cavrix-ai-team**
- Responsibilities: `ai/`, Local LLM daemon orchestration, API/D-Bus integrations.

### Packaging & Release Engineering
- **@cavrix-release-eng**
- Responsibilities: `packages/`, CI/CD pipelines, ISO builds, Mirrorlist maintenance.

## Becoming a Maintainer
Maintainer status is granted to active contributors who have submitted significant PRs over a 6-month period and demonstrated adherence to the project's Engineering Specifications.
